Restaurants in Cambridge (UK) and beyond

Here is my personal list of favorite restaurants in the Cambridge area (and some in London) if you happen to be in the area and you are looking for a culinary exploration. Needless to say, it's a matter of taste. Enjoy!
Gourmet House

45 Castle St, Cambridge CB3 0AH, United Kingdom

A nice Chinese restaurant with authentic recipes. A few minutes outside of the city center.

The Chinese Canteen

30 Bridge St, Cambridge CB2 1UJ, United Kingdom

Close to the Cam at the border of the old city centre.

Hong Kong Fusion

12 St Johns St, Cambridge CB2 1TW, United Kingdom

A small Chinese restaurant right across the street from St. Johns College.

Cafe Oriential Dumpling Bar

9 Burleigh St, Cambridge CB1 1DG, United Kingdom

Near the Grafton Centre.

Mr Ho Chinese Restaurant

54 Mill Rd, Cambridge CB1 2AS, United Kingdom

On the Millroad coming from Parker's Piece. Very authentic.

Spring Restaurant

66 Mill Rd, Cambridge CB1 2AS, United Kingdom

Another one on the Millroad coming from Parker's Piece.

Japas Sushi Restaurant

9 Saxon St, Cambridge CB2 1HN, United Kingdom

Slightly hidden along an old channel. Very good selection of Sake.

Yim Wah Express

2-4 Lensfield Rd, Cambridge CB2 1EG, United Kingdom

At the crossing at The Church of Our Lady and the English Martyrs and in walking distance from Parker's piece.

Seven Days

66 Regent St, Cambridge CB2 1DP, United Kingdom

Right at one of the central food streets of Cambridge's city centre, a good selection of authentic Chinese food.

The Old Street Chinese Restaurant

186 Old St, Old Street, London EC1V 9FR, United Kingdom

One of the best Chinese places in London. Tastes like in China. Easy to reach with the Northern Line coming from King's Cross / St. Pancras or from London Bridge.

Din Tai Fung

5 Henrietta St, Covent Garden, London WC2E 8PT, United Kingdom

Taiwanese Dumpling restaurant. A bit on the pricy side. Located right across Covenant Garden.
