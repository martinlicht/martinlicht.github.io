Travel preparation and checklist

This is my travel checklist and lifehack collection for traveling to conferences and similar events. I have put this travel checklist online for primarily for my personal ease of access, but I am happy if it is helpful to whomever else happens to read it!

Bags and luggage

  • Hand luggage
    Any small bag that you can carry with you. Contains items that you must keep securely with you!
  • Carry-on luggage
    Items of secondary importance that can manage losing. If the airline forces you to check in your carry-on, you will not have access to it during the flight. In addition, the airline may lose your carry-on.
  • Bigger luggage
    Any other items that could manage losing.
For each piece of luggage, prepare a sheet of paper with Lost-and-Found information. The Lost-and-Found information should contain:
  • Your name
  • Email and phone number
  • Flight number
  • Travel address
  • Home address
(how to contact you, flight number, etc.), and deposit it in each item.

Travel Preparations

Destination Research

  • Read Wikitravel entry for the country/state
  • Read Wikitravel entry for the city
  • Safety conditions at destination, city areas to avoid
  • Official travel advisories and warnings
  • Vaccinations (check required and recommended vaccinations)
  • Visa requirements and entry restrictions
  • Local laws and regulations
  • Local customs and etiquette
  • Weather conditions during visit
  • Opening hours of grocery stores

Finances

  • What are the local currency and the exchange rates?
  • Credit/debit cards and their limitations

Accommodation

  • Hotel/hostel reservations
  • Check-in/check-out times
  • Contact information for the accommodation

Transportation

  • Flight/train/bus tickets and their prices
  • Transportation between destinations (e.g., car rental, local transportation options)
  • Airport transfers
  • Maps and directions

Communication and Technology

  • Internet access options (phone provider)
  • Language translation help and tools
  • Emergency contact details
  • Sufficient space on phone for photos and videos

Entertainment

  • What are the sights and tourist destinations that you want to visit?
  • What are the local food options?

Packing categories, listed in descending order of importance

Travel documents and essentials

  • Wallet
  • Credit/Debit cards and cash
  • Cash for emergencies
  • Travel itinerary and reservations
  • Passport / ID
  • Visa, permits, printouts of all relevant immigration documents
  • Printout of event invitation
  • Vaccinations (check required and recommended vaccinations)
  • Travel-size umbrella
  • Travel insurance documents (if needed)
  • International driving permit (if needed)

Health

  • Prescription glasses and glasses cloth (if applicable)
  • Contact lenses and solution (if applicable)
  • Prescription medications
  • Over-the-counter medications (pain relievers, allergy medication, etc.)
  • Sunscreen
  • Travel insurance (medical coverage)
  • Insect repellent
  • First aid kit
  • Emergency contact information

Toiletries and Personal Care Items

  • Zip bags to deposit these items
  • Toothbrush and toothpaste
  • Shampoo and conditioner
  • Soap or body wash
  • Deodorant
  • Razor and shaving cream
  • nail clipper
  • band aids
  • desinfectant
  • Hairbrush or comb
  • Hair styling, face wash, skincare products
  • Tissues

Electronics

  • Power adapters and converters
  • Mobile phone and charger
  • Laptop and charger
  • Laptop mouse
  • USB stick
  • Earphones (and case, if applicable)
  • General-purpose USB cables
  • Power banks
  • Camera and memory cards

Work

  • Folder with relevant work documents and sheets of paper
  • Pen/pencil

Clothing and accessories

  • Tops: shirts and T-Shirts
  • Bottoms: trousers (long/short)
  • Socks and undergarments
  • Warm top (sweater, etc.) on body as back-up
  • Jacket appropriate to weather
  • Belt
  • Scarf
  • Sunglasses
  • Swimwear (if applicable)
  • Sportswear and sports shoes (if applicable)

Miscellaneous:

  • Passport photos (e.g., for public transport passes)
  • Reusable shopping bag
  • Water bottle
  • Snacks
  • Luggage tags and locks
  • Travel-sized laundry detergent (if needed)
  • Fork, knife, spoon (check flight regulations)
  • ....

After return:

  • Stash spare change
  • Laundry all cloth (as necessary)
